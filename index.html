RAJ MIND HEALTH SERVICES

If a Child can't learn the way we teach , may be, we should teach the way they learn. - Ignacio Estrada

We at MHS, in collaboration with MDA - Madras Dyslexia Association , offer a conducive and necessary environment for those children with Specific Learning Disability ( Dyslexia) to overcome their difficulty.

One to one attention is given by a trained special educator with Individualised Educational Plan and Unique Lesson Plan . Each and every step of the child's progress is monitored very closely and all the required inputs are provided to the child to overcome the learning disability at ease with time and age appropriate counselling.

Constant and Systematic follow up by our knowledge partner MDA adds more value and quality to the service rendered and the outcome as well.

Who We Are ?

Mrs. Sumathi Sivakumar, the founder Director of Raj Mind Health Services is a Post Graduate in Psychology from the Madras University with 15 years of total professional experience at different capacities in Chennai, Hyderabad, Germany & U.S.A. Her specialisation in Psychology has Certification in Marriage, Family and Child Counselling under renowned ACE Program of The Chennai Counselor's Foundation. Her certifications also include Couple Therapy, Counselling in Educational Institutions, Corporate Counselling, Hypnotherapy, Career Guidance, Transactional Analysis, Psychometric Assessments etc.

She has also completed her Teacher Training Program at the LMOIS and had intensive hands on training on Handling Children with Specific Disability - Dyslexia at the MDA with experience in handling such children at a renowned school at Kunrathur, Chennai.

What We Do ?

Raj MIND HEALTH SERVICES is a centre for Special Education, Counselling Services, Career Guidance, Assessments and Training .

MHS, Focus on Overall Child Academic Development especially for Children with Specific Learning Disability ( Dyslexia). MHS believes that "A Child with Dyslexia is only one part of him/her,it does not define the child".

MHS also renders counselling services on issues related to childhood, Adolescence, Behavioural Problems and Parenting. Assessments are also carried out for identifying Child's IQ, Symptoms of ADHD, Autism, Hyperactivity, Anxiety Disorders etc.
